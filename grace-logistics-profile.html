About Us

Your Trusted Partner in Global Logistics

At Grace Logistics, we are redefining the logistics landscape in Sri Lanka and beyond. Launched in September 2023, we've rapidly built a strong, loyal customer base by delivering ethical, efficient, and customer-focused supply chain solutions.

We specialize in import and export logistics, helping businesses navigate complex global trade with ease. From freight forwarding to customs clearance, our multi-skilled operations, customer service, and sales teams are committed to excellence — ensuring every shipment is handled with precision and care.

Backed by 20–30 years of leadership experience, we operate with the agility of a startup and the confidence of seasoned professionals. Our clients work directly with our top management, allowing us to deliver better value, faster decisions, and tailored solutions with an unmatched level of trust and accountability.
