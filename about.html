Get to know us

Grace Logistics is a leading logistics and supply chain management company based in Colombo, Sri Lanka. Established in 2023, we have rapidly grown to offer a comprehensive suite of services.

Grace Logistics was founded by a team of seasoned shipping and freight forwarding industry veterans with over 25 years of experience. Recognizing the need for a customer-centric logistics partner in Sri Lanka, we established Grace Logistics as a one-stop shop fulfilling all transportation and cargo delivery requirements.

Within the first year, we grew our international network to cover key global markets and added essential domestic distribution capabilities. We are now positioned as a top innovative logistics company in Sri Lanka.
