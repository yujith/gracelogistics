Overview

Executive Summary

This proposal outlines the development of a custom digital freight rate platform for Grace Logistics — a leading logistics and supply chain provider in Sri Lanka. The system will empower Grace Logistics to publish live freight rates on their existing website, allow customers to search and request bookings in real time, and provide administrators with a secure, full-featured dashboard to manage rates, ports, users, container types, commodity types, and pricing tiers — no technical knowledge required.

The platform replaces manual quoting processes with an always-on, automated system, positioning Grace Logistics as a technology-first freight forwarder in a competitive market. The project will be fully delivered and live on gracelogisticslk.com by 20 March 2026.
